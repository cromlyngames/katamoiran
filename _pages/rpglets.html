Games I've written, all of which fit on one letter-sized page (though you might have to squint a little if you print them -- I've never tried). They're listed here in the order I created them, so paint a pretty good picture of my evolving design philosophy (and improvement with LaTex).

6 Hours to Midnight

... and no one is precisely who they seem to be, including you.

Intrigue and romance in a time-based format designed to create a story. Inspired by S\lay with w/Me. Intended to encourage exploration of themes of love, sex, and rivalry in a rpg context.

Probably suitable for solo play only, though you could possibly play with a GM and one or two players, maybe even competitively. It's a little fiddly with a somewhat lavish amount of things to keep track of, very story focused, and not at all suitable for long-term play.

6 Against the Dark / 6 Days to Adventure

Two flavors of essentially the same game; one is horror of the haunted house variety and the other hearkens back to the big globe-trotting, treasure hunting adventure potboilers that are ubiquitous at airport kiosks.

Built around a board-game-style three Act structure with a simple dice resolution and options to purchase on each roll. Still pretty much for one shots, though there's more room in these for group play.

6 Feet Under

Mystery with a hard-boiled, noir feel, written after *6 Against the Dark* as I was transitioning away from a "purchasing outcomes" framework and towards Apocalypse World's resolution system.

Uses a character generation method similar to that of 6 Against the Dark, but replaces the standard three Act structure with one inspired by a "beat sheet" for a more fluid but still very board-game-like result.

Secrets & Shadows / Starfarer

The former is paranormal romance, or urban fantasy, if you prefer, while the latter is straight science fiction. Draws heaviy on Lady Blackbird for character generation and Apocalypse World for resolution and mechancis.

No built-in structure or end point, but plenty of color and situation starters to get things going and keep them moving. Should be playable solo or in a group, one shot or long term.
